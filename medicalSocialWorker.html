MEDICAL SOCIAL WORKER

Medical Social Workers help resolve social or emotional problems that are going on in the home. MSW will assess & act on your needs and your family to access community resources and social services. Medicare will pay 100% for intermittent skilled Medical Social Worker (MSW) if the patient is home bound and the services are ordered by a physician.

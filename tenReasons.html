10 REASONS TO CONTACT EASTWEST

1) Organized

All of EastWest’s staff are put on laptops. Our Intake Coordinators submit every order into our computer system. Our Director of Nurse oversees all nursing and therapy visits, regulates Medicare guidelines, answer all clinical questions and reads all orders. Every case is well thought out to give you the Best Patient Care.

2) Compassionate

If you don’t have compassion in what you do in life then you better STOP now. We at EastWest hire staff who truly care and treat you like family. Each member of EastWest wants to help people and are very experienced home health care professionals.

3) Dignity

The EastWest Family is highly respected in the community and has been in Commerce Township for over 50 years. We take pride to represent physicians, lawyers, families, hospitals, nursing homes, assisted livings and rehab centers.

4) Respect

No matter what health condition or financial status you are in, WE RESPECT YOU and guide your family in the right direction.

5) Family Oriented

EastWest is family owned since 2006. We always stick together and want the best home care experience for your family.

6) Ethical

EastWest is Medicare Certified and licensed by the State of Michigan. Every staff member of EastWest follows all insurance and state guidelines. Our caregivers are drug screened, background checked, and trained in our training room.

7) Caring

We will come visit you at the hospital, rehab, nursing facility and in the comfort of your home. Have your doctor or discharge planner contact us.

8) Motivating

Our team works hard everyday to spread the word about EastWest. You can find us Online, Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, Instagram, Newsletters, Senior Care Presentations and Sponsored Events.

9) Available

24/7 we pick up our phones .Our staff is always on call after 5pm. You can message us online and we will respond the same day.

10) Safe

Every staff member at EastWest are background checked and fingered printed. We know how important it is to feel safe at home. Our staff members are there to improve your quality of life at home. We provide training and education opportunities to all members at EastWest.
